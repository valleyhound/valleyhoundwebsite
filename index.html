About

We are a small British independent game studio & publisher creating quality PC games.

Our current flagship is Wyoming Winter which was released in December 2022 on Steam. It is a multiplayer arcade horror game in which a group of department of defence employees sneak around an office complex in hopes of finding a defence against the entity stalking them through the hallways.

We honestly strive to only publish games we ourselves find fun. Origional, fun experiences from the start.


Our In-House Games

Wyoming Winter - Steam Publication

Published in 2022 to itch.io before being made accessible on Steam December 9th the same year, Wyoming Winter is our current flagship in-house development and represents more than two years of development & refinement.



Enter & Survey - Tech Demo / Upcoming Steam Publication

Initially produced as a game jam entry, Enter & Survey quickly became a ambient horror favourite on itch.io thanks to its unique art style and mechanics. E&S is currently under development for a future full Steam release.



Our Products

ValleyHound Base - A Complete Standard for Unity Prototyping

ValleyHound Base is a complete standard development toolset for the Unity game engine designed with ValleyHound Game's workflows. Becoming available for clients soon it will be a showcase of internal tools and demonstrate our processes for game development. ValleyHound Base is built with premade internal development tools, assets and workflows. A key part of VHBase is VHB oversight which keeps track of the scene and assets to ensure that tight rules and workflows are maintained to avoid sloppy work.
