About

ValleyHound Games is a British game development company specializing in PC games. We work on creating high-quality and engaging games that immerse players in fleshed-out unique play spaces which have yet to be seen before.

Our portfolio lists successful projects such as Wyoming Winter, Enter & Survey, and more, We're trusted for quality and maintainability across our client produced and in-house projects. Our focus on utilizing internal prestructured assets, workflows and codebases in our projects allows us to bring innovative and dynamic gameplay experiences to life with ease.

At ValleyHound Games, we believe that games should be high-quality, unique and accessible from the start and so we strive to create games that follow these principles closely.


Our In-House Games

Wyoming Winter - Steam Publication

Published in 2022 to itch.io before being made accessible on Steam December 9th the same year, Wyoming Winter is our current flagship in-house development and represents more than two years of refinement in workflow processes for VH Games.



Enter & Survey - Tech Demo / Upcoming Steam Publication

Initially produced as a tech demo for many point rendering Enter & Survey quickly became a ambient horror favourite on itch.io thanks to its unique art style and mechanics. E&S is currently under development for a future full Steam release.



Our Products

VHBase - A Complete Standard for Unity Prototyping

VHB is a complete standard development toolset for the Unity game engine designed with ValleyHound Game's workflows and ideology in mind. Becoming available for clients soon it will be a showcase of internal tools and demonstrate the VH processes for game development. VHBase is built with premade internal development tools, assets and workflows. A key part of VHBase is VHB Overlord which keeps track of the scene and assets to ensure that tight rules and workflows are maintained to avoid sloppy work. This project is a 2023 development and was developed after the release of some in house projects.
