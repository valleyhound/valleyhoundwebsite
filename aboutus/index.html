An independent British games studio working on great games.

Based in the UK, ValleyHound Games is a small independent game studio currently working on our flagship game, Wyoming Winter.

We're a Steamworks partner and self publish to Steam under the ValleyHound Games publisher account.


ValleyHound Games was founded in March 2022 to publish Wyoming Winter after the game was in development for over a year.

We started on itch.io and moved to Steam in December 2022.


We make the games we play.

When it comes to the type of games we make, unless we are developing for a client we only make games that we would play casually ourselves. That means only making games we ourselves find fun.

It is the core tenant for how we select the ideas we want to work on.
